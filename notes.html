To transform your tabs to other key enter your tabs here:

P.S. You can write either "Do" or "C", register doesn't matter. To specify tabs (sharp or flat) use "+" and "-".
Attention! Don't use Enter in text input. If you paste text tabs with paragraphs, delete them. The app supports 3 octaves: 3, 4, 5. If not specified, it will be the 4th one. Example of input:
"Sol, G3 sol-, sol+, sol Sol♭~4 Sol♭5"

Now choose the origin key and to what key you want to change the melody
